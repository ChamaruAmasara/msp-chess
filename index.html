About MoraSpirit

MoraSpirit, the leading light of university sports is in its way to create a full of pride, highly passionate sporting culture at university level in Sri Lanka. More than a website MoraSpirit acts as a social network itself, gathering university community together to forming a university sports value. MSp focused broadly on make out all the university sportsmen and women in an unbiased manner. Thanks to an enthusiastic crew, MSp has turn into the destination of choice for anyone wanting to catch up on the latest university sports news.
